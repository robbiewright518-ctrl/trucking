# Advanced Trucking System

A feature-rich trucking job system designed for immersive gameplay, progression, and both legal and underground careers.

---

## Job Variety

### Legal Jobs (25+ Types)
- Local Deliveries
- Long Haul Routes
- Fragile Cargo Transport
- Fuel Tankers
- Livestock Transport
- Luxury Vehicle Hauling
- Medical Supplies
- Hazardous Materials
- And many more...

### Underground Jobs (10 Types)
- Narcotics Transport
- Weapons Trafficking
- Organ Transport
- Cartel Deliveries
- High-risk illegal contracts

> Includes a separate criminal reputation progression system

---

## Progression & Rewards

### Ranking System
- 50-Level progression system
- Unlock higher-paying and more advanced jobs

### Criminal Career Track
- 30 underground ranks
- Progress from **Street Runner** to **Kingpin**

### Achievements
- 12+ unlockable achievements:
  - Distance traveled
  - Jobs completed
  - Total earnings

### Perks (6 Unlockable)
- Increased payouts
- Fuel efficiency boost
- Damage resistance
- XP boost
